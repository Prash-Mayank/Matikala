Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how we collect and use your data.

Last updated: January 2026

Summary: Matikala collects only the information necessary to process your orders and improve your experience. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Who We Are

Matikala ("we", "us", "our") is a handcrafted clay sculpture brand based in India, operating through our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, create an account, or place an order.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you have any questions, please contact us at [email protected].

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account information — name, email address, phone number, and password when you register.
  • Order information — delivery address, billing details, and order history when you place a purchase.
  • Payment information — payment is processed securely by Razorpay. We do not store card numbers, UPI IDs, or banking credentials on our servers.
  • Communications — messages, enquiries, or feedback you send us via email, contact form, or WhatsApp.
  • Reviews — if you leave a product review, your name and review content are stored.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, clicks, and navigation patterns collected via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Device information — browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and IP address.
  • Location data — approximate location inferred from IP address (not GPS).

How We Use Your Information

Order Fulfilment

Processing your orders, arranging delivery, sending tracking updates, and handling returns or refunds.

Account Management

Creating and maintaining your account, managing your wishlist, addresses, and order history.

Communications

Sending order confirmations, shipping updates, and responding to your queries and support requests.

Improving Our Service

Analysing usage patterns to improve our website, product listings, and shopping experience.

Security & Fraud Prevention

Detecting and preventing fraudulent transactions, unauthorised access, and misuse of our platform.

Legal Compliance

Meeting our obligations under Indian law, including GST reporting and consumer protection regulations.

Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share it only with trusted partners necessary to operate our business:

Partner Purpose Data Shared
Razorpay Payment processing Order amount, transaction reference
Delivery partners (Delhivery, DTDC, etc.) Order shipping & delivery Name, delivery address, phone number
Firebase / Google Platform hosting, authentication, database Account data, order data
Google Analytics Website usage analytics Anonymised usage data, device info
Email / SMS providers Order notifications, OTP delivery Email address, phone number

We may also disclose your data if required by law, court order, or government authority.

Data Retention

  • Account data — retained as long as your account is active, plus 2 years after deletion for legal compliance.
  • Order data — retained for 7 years as required by Indian GST and accounting laws.
  • Communication logs — retained for 2 years.
  • Analytics data — anonymised and retained for up to 26 months.

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. Certain data required by law cannot be deleted before the mandatory retention period expires.

Your Rights

As a user of our platform, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or outdated information in your account.

Deletion

Request deletion of your account and personal data (subject to legal retention requirements).

Opt Out

Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in any email we send.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at [email protected] with the subject line "Data Request". We'll respond within 10 business days.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption on all pages
  • Firebase security rules restricting data access by user role
  • Razorpay PCI-DSS compliant payment processing — we never store payment credentials
  • Regular security audits and access control reviews

Note: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use a strong password and do not share your account credentials.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, keep you logged in, and understand how visitors use our site. For full details, please see our Cookie Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, for significant changes, notify registered users via email. Your continued use of our website after changes are published constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
