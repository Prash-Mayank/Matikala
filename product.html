The Twin Spirit Vessel is a study in duality — two organic forms intertwined, each incomplete without the other. Handcrafted from locally sourced Rajasthani clay, this piece carries the quiet confidence of traditional Indian artisanship elevated into the contemporary home.

Design & Aesthetic

  • Dual-form silhouette inspired by classical Indian vessel traditions
  • Organic, asymmetric profile — no two pieces are identical
  • Subtle surface texturing from hand-applied clay slip
  • Harmonious proportions designed for modern minimalist spaces
  • Natural matte finish that evolves beautifully over time

Craftsmanship

  • Hand-thrown and hand-sculpted by master artisans
  • Rajasthani red clay, kiln-fired at 1200°C for lasting strength
  • Each piece takes 3–4 days to complete end-to-end
  • Quality-checked and signed by the artisan
  • Authenticity certificate included with every order
"Two vessels, one spirit. This piece was born from a simple observation — that all great things exist in relationship. The twin forms lean on each other, support each other, breathe together."
— Shivank Singh, Founder & Creative Director, Matikala

Care Instructions

Avoid prolonged water exposure Dust with a soft dry cloth Keep away from direct heat Handle with both hands Not dishwasher safe
